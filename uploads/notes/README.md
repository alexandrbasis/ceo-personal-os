# Notes

Place any supporting documents, journal entries, or reference materials here.

## What to Upload

- Performance reviews from work
- 360 feedback documents
- Coaching or therapy notes
- Journal entries worth preserving
- Notes from meaningful conversations
- Reference documents for your goals
- Anything else that informs your self-understanding

## How to Use These

These documents serve as raw material for:

1. Pattern recognition during annual reviews
2. Refreshing your memory on past insights
3. Grounding your self-assessment in real feedback
4. Understanding your evolution over time

## File Naming Convention

Suggested: `[YYYY-MM]_[description].md`

Example:
- `2023-06_performance_review.pdf`
- `2024-01_coaching_session_notes.md`
- `2022-12_360_feedback.docx`

---

*These are private documents. Only upload what you're comfortable having in this system.*
