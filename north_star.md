# North Star

Your North Star is not a goal. It's a direction. Goals can be achieved and checked off. A North Star is the orientation that makes all your goals make sense.

This document holds one thing: **what matters most to you right now, in this season of your life.**

## The Test

When you're unsure about a decision, ask:

**"Does this move me toward my North Star, away from it, or is it neutral?"**

- Toward: Default yes
- Away: Default no
- Neutral: Consider opportunity cost

Not everything has to connect to your North Star. But the things that take significant time or energy should.
